i need to take risks or else i’m going to die

cars driving by your house sound like waves on the beach
in fiji or something
except they honk and play music
and sound nothing like waves on the beach
both of them can kill small children
i don’t know where fiji is on a map

i have technology in development
that will cause me to self-immolate
if i put on “work appropriate attire”
and i’m deliberating over shades of brushed steel
for my bathroom appliances
and i’m going to concede
to the pressures of corporate professionalism
>
